About the Anura Meter

The Anura Meter tracks the progress of selected proposals in President Anura Kumara Dissanayake's 2024 Presidential Election manifesto. The selection is focused on a set of promises that is thought to have high public interest. The progress is updated as and when we receive information from the government or from any other sources. We welcome any assistance that you could provide in improving what is tracked and in updating progress.

Key Areas of Focus

Economic Reform

focus on investment, trade, public finance, and tax reforms

Governance

processes and systems of decision-making, coordination, and management within the government for better transparency and accountability

Anti-Corruption

implementing systems and legal frameworks to prevent, detect, and punish corrupt practices within public institutions and government operations

Social Protection

policies, programs, and regulatory mechanisms aimed at reducing poverty, vulnerability, and discrimination

Law and Order

delivering justice for past political violence, ensuring accountability for high-profile crimes, and upholding the rule of law

Progress Statuses
No update
No visible action has been taken or recorded by any official source considered.
Initiated
Action has been taken into consideration through a budget allocation or cabinet decision.
Partially kept
Steps have been taken to approve the action which is visible through i.e. a cabinet decision or gazetted bill, but has not been fully executed.
Kept
The promise has been fully implemented.
Broken
The promise has been contradicted or actively reversed.

What are official government sources?

Our sources are official government documents and publicly accessible records that provide verified information on progress made on the promises by the government. Some of these sources include, but are not limited to, cabinet decisions, budget documents, gazetted documents, acts, annual reports, and data from government websites. However, we do not consider statements provided by public officials as official sources.

Disclaimers of the Initiative

The 22 promises that were selected were based on our observations on the most critical elements of the manifesto that generated high public interest.

This is a best effort initiative, where small mistakes and oversights are possible. We seek to crowdsource correction for any inadvertent errors.
