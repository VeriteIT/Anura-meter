About the Anura Meter

Anura Meter tracks selected manifesto promises of President Anura Kumara Dissanayake based on their alignment with key areas of public interest, relating to economic reform, governance, anti-corruption, social protection, and law and order.

Areas of Focus

Economic Reform

Focus on investment, trade, public finance, and tax reforms.

Governance

Address processes and systems of decision-making, coordination, and management within the government for better transparency and accountability.

Anti-Corruption

Focus on implementing systems and legal frameworks to prevent, detect, and punish corrupt practices within public institutions and government operations.

Social Protection

Refer to policies, programs, and regulatory mechanisms aimed at reducing poverty, vulnerability, and discrimination.

Law and Order

Focus on delivering justice for past political violence, ensuring accountability for high-profile crimes, and upholding the rule of law.

Data Sources

  • Publicly available information from official government sources
  • Government responses to Right to Information (RTI) requests
  • Crowd-sourced updates verified by the Parliamentary Research Team at Verité Research

Disclaimers of the Initiative

The 24 promises that were selected were based on our observations on the most critical elements of the manifesto that generated high public interest.

This is a best effort initiative, where small mistakes and oversights are possible. We seek to crowdsource correction for any inadvertent errors.

The inclusion or omission of a promise on this platform should not be considered an endorsement.
